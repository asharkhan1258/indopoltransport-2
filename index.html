Our Services

At Alexis Freight & Transport LLC, we offer several transportation solutions to meet your shipping and hauling needs. Choose the option that best fits your applications:

Freight Shipping

We provide freight shipping for loads of various sizes. Solutions such as trailer shipping, drive-away services and hotshot trucking are available for domestic and international transport.

Heavy Hauling

Our heavy equipment hauling services include transporting oversize shipments. We map out shipment plans and obtain pilot cars when necessary for improved safety.

Auto Transportation

We offer auto transport solutions for cars of any size. You can choose between drive-away and trailer shipping services for safe vehicle delivery.

Flatbed Trucking

Flatbed trucking is perfect for transporting heavy machinery, construction equipment, mining equipment and oversized loads across the United States.

Agriculture Equipment

Agriculture companies and farmers use our equipment and commodity transportation solutions to move farm equipment and export commodities.

Power-Only Transportation

If you supply the trailer, we can haul it. Our team finds the best driver for your route and handles the required paperwork.

Customer Experience

Alexis Freight & Transport LLC assigns a dedicated representative to each order. That agent takes your order, dispatches the shipment, tracks the load and verifies delivery. Your representative also ensures that our shipment plan matches your preferences and solves communication problems during transportation.

Instead of dealing with various departments of other logistics agencies, you'll build a relationship with an individual, knowledgeable agent.

We ask a comprehensive list of questions before arranging your transportation. Knowing this information ahead of time lets us develop a seamless delivery process. When complications occur, we have the details we need to help get your shipment back on track.
