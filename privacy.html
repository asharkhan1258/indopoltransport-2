Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters to Us

Last updated: May 21, 2026

IPT Service is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use https://www.indopoltransport.online (the “Website”), how we use it, how we protect it, and the rights you have regarding your personal information.

If you do not agree with this Policy, please do not access or use our Website.

1. What Personal Data We Collect

  • Contact Information – Name, email address, telephone number, company, and mailing address.
  • Business Information – Information related to your organization or services requested.
  • Account Data – Information tied to registered user accounts (if applicable).
  • Location & Technical Information – IP address, browser type, device identifiers, operating system, and browsing activity.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies – Data about site usage, analytics, and preferences.
  • Communications – Records of inquiries, support requests, and feedback.
  • Email Interconnectivity – Data on when/if emails are opened and links clicked.
  • Media Submissions – Uploaded images or media (including metadata such as location).
  • Sensitive Information – Only if expressly provided by you (e.g., dietary, accessibility, or health needs).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and manage the services you request.
  • Process event registrations and administer accounts.
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests.
  • Analyze site usage and improve website functionality.
  • Send relevant updates, newsletters, and marketing (with consent where required).
  • Personalize content and communications.
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud or misuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

3. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate our Website and analyze use, including essential, functional, and analytics cookies.

Third-party cookies (e.g., YouTube or social media) may also be used.

4. Who We Share Your Data With

We may share data only with service providers, affiliates, or as required by law.

We do not sell your information.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for business or legal purposes, typically up to one year for analytics and six months for contact forms.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data, and to withdraw consent at any time.

7. Security

We use secure servers, encryption, and limited access to protect your information.

In the event of a breach, we will notify affected users as required.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our Website and services are not directed toward children under 18.

We do not knowingly collect their information.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Changes will be reflected on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.
